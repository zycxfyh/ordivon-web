Useful context

Make the discussion easy to act on.

Include the relevant page, file, workflow, claim, or research question; describe the expected result and the observed result; and link supporting evidence where possible. A focused report can move directly into review and implementation.

Security

Share sensitive reports safely.

Keep passwords, API keys, authentication tokens, recovery codes, personal financial data, and active exploit details out of public issues. Identify the affected repository and request a private reporting route, then follow its `SECURITY.md` guidance where available.
