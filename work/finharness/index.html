Capital system · Active engineering

Review material capital decisions from verified state.

FinHarness is a local-first personal capital review and decision system. It mirrors capital evidence into receipt-backed state, derives bounded observations and candidates, supports governed human review, and keeps execution on a simulated substrate.

Product journey

Turn imported evidence into a reviewable decision.

01Trusted capital state

Import contract-compliant CSV or Beancount evidence into a receipt-backed state model.

02Admitted evidence

Expose truth readiness, observations, exposures, and bounded research inputs.

03Decision candidate

Build a reviewable proposal with rationale, risks, and explicit boundaries.

04Human judgment

Decide, defer, or reject before any simulated execution path proceeds.

05Outcome review

Reconcile what happened and preserve evidence for later learning.

Current surface

A bounded system, not an autonomous capital manager.

Capital evidence

Read-only imports, receipt-backed state, truth-readiness reporting, exposure, and Daily Brief surfaces.

Decision review

Governed proposal generation, local human review, and a separate loopback-only review mode.

Agent participation

Bounded read, explain, and candidate-producing surfaces under FinHarness-owned admission, authority, receipts, and stop conditions.

Explicit non-capabilities

No real broker SDK, funded account, external venue submission, transfer, tax submission, or delegated autonomous capital management.
