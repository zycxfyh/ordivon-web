Status language

Different stages should look different.

Active engineering means current implementation work is occurring. Foundational means a project supplies durable ideas or mechanisms but may not receive continuous feature work. Research foundation means the question, method, and evidence base are still being constructed. Experimental infrastructure means useful and public, but not a production service.
