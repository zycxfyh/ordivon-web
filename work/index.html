Portfolio

Each project advances a different layer.

Ordivon Core develops the shared method. FinHarness applies that method to consequential capital decisions. Ordinary Prosperity Research studies the economic mechanisms and institutions that shape long-term choice. Ordivon Web connects the work through public publishing and inspectable records.

Together, they form a practical path from ideas to systems, from systems to evidence, and from evidence to better future work.
