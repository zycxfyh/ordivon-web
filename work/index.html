Portfolio

The projects exchange evidence, methods, and lessons—not ownership.

Each repository remains independently understandable and owns its current technical truth. Ordivon Runtime can support engineering work without becoming a FinHarness component. FinHarness can apply useful execution and recovery patterns without turning its financial domain model into general infrastructure. Ordinary Prosperity remains a research program rather than a product dependency.

Ordivon Web connects these surfaces through concise public explanations while avoiding a second mutable registry of repository facts.
